Big Bites
Logo Design

Sketching

A logo created for a brand new company. Big Bites breeds various rare bugs to provide healthy diets for reptiles big and small. The clients main goal was to have a highly recognizable brand. It was also important to include the most popular products in the logo - fruit flies and isopods.

Digital Logo

Once roughs have been explored, the shapes are built in illustrator. Both bugs are included in the logo. Using them as seperate entities from the logo will aid brand recognition as deisred.
