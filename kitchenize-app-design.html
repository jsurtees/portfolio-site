App Design
Kitchenize

About

Kitchenize is a smart kitchen organizer and tool that automates a kitchen and enables foodies to be organized, productive, and creative in the kitchen. It contains daily updated recipes that can be saved and followed with a smart watch. It can connect with your smart appliances like a fridge, oven or stove for gesture or phone control.

Ideation/Sketching

The problem statement: Foodies need a way to be more efficient in the kitchen because their kitchens are unorganized. The app definition statement: Kitchenize is a smart kitchen organizer and tool that automates a kitchen and enables foodies to be organized, productive, and creative in the kitchen.

Bulding Screens

Now a problem and user have been defined, flow is mapped out, and pages are sketched. The sketches are put into Xd and brought to life using the material guidelines. Many screens were created to have a properly functioning prototype.
